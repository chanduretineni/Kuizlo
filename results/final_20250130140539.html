Final Document: Editing for Style, Usage, and Grammar with Generative AI

1. Introduction

This document outlines the process of editing a business email for style, usage, and grammar using generative AI tools. The activity aims to maintain human agency and authorship while leveraging AI capabilities to enhance the quality of written communication.

2. Task Completion Outline

2.1 Task Overview

The assignment involves several tasks, from reading the sample email to identifying and editing compositional errors using AI tools. The tasks are designed to ensure a thorough analysis and improvement of the email content.

2.2 Detailed Tasks and Subtasks

  • Task t1: Read the sample email provided in the assignment.
    • Identify the subject line.
    • Assess content clarity.
    • Check for adherence to formatting guidelines.
  • Task t2: Use a generative AI tool to identify errors in style, usage, and grammar in the email.
    • Enter the email into ChatGPT.
    • Apply provided style guides and rules.
    • Review AI-generated error list.
  • Task t3: Enter the AI Prompting Protocol instruction to identify compositional errors in the email.
    • Instruction: "Identify errors in style, usage, and grammar."
    • Paste the email below the instruction.
    • Submit the prompt to AI.
  • Task t4: Cut-and-paste the identified errors generated by AI into a Word document.
    • List errors in the order they appear.
    • Save the document.
  • Task t5: Use the AI tool to make suggested edits to the identified compositional errors.
    • Edit only critical errors affecting clarity.
    • Ensure AI does not rewrite the entire email.
  • Task t6: Cut-and-paste the suggested edits generated by AI into the same Word document next to the original sentences.
    • Present edits next to the original sentences.
    • Ensure correct formatting and alignment.
  • Task t7: Submit the Word document to the Moodle portal under 'Editing for Style, Usage, Grammar with AI.'
    • Review the Word document for formatting.
    • Log into the Moodle portal.
    • Upload and submit the document.

3. User Answers Integration

3.1 Key Elements of the Sample Email

The key elements to focus on in the sample email include the subject line, content clarity, and adherence to formatting guidelines.

3.2 Generative AI Tool Selection

ChatGPT was chosen as the generative AI tool for identifying errors in the email, following the provided guidelines.

3.3 AI Prompting Protocol Instruction

The specific instruction used was: "Identify errors in style, usage, and grammar."

3.4 Organization of Identified Errors

Errors were listed in the Word document in the order they appeared in the email.

3.5 Criteria for Editing Compositional Errors

Only critical errors affecting clarity were targeted for edits.

3.6 Presentation of Suggested Edits

Suggested edits were presented next to the original sentences in the Word document.

3.7 Document Formatting and Submission

The Word document was reviewed for correct formatting and double-checked before submission to the Moodle portal.

4. Conclusion

The integration of generative AI in editing for style, usage, and grammar enhances the quality of written communication while maintaining human oversight. This document systematically addressed all tasks, providing a comprehensive guide to improving business email communication.
