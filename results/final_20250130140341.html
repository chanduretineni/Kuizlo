Final Document: Editing for Style, Usage, and Grammar with Generative AI

1. Introduction

This document outlines the systematic process of editing a sample business email using generative AI tools to identify and correct errors in style, usage, and grammar. The aim is to maintain human agency and authorship over the message while ensuring clarity and professionalism in business communication.

2. Task Completion Outline

  1. Task 1: Reading the Sample Email

    Focus on subject line, content clarity, and formatting guidelines.
    • Identify the subject line.
    • Assess content clarity.
    • Check for adherence to formatting guidelines.

  2. Task 2: Using AI to Identify Errors

    Using ChatGPT with provided guidelines.
    • Enter the email into ChatGPT.
    • Apply provided style guides and rules.
    • Review AI-generated error list.

  3. Task 3: AI Prompting Protocol Instruction

    Instruction: Identify errors in style, usage, and grammar.
    • Enter the specific AI Prompting Protocol instruction.
    • Paste the email below the instruction.
    • Submit the prompt to AI.

  4. Task 4: Documenting Identified Errors

    List errors in the order they appear.
    • Open a Word document.
    • Paste AI-generated errors in order.
    • Save the document.

  5. Task 5: Making Suggested Edits

    Edit only critical errors affecting clarity.
    • Enter the prompt for suggested edits.
    • Review AI suggestions for critical errors.
    • Ensure AI does not rewrite the entire email.

  6. Task 6: Presenting Suggested Edits

    Present edits next to the original sentences.
    • Open the Word document with errors.
    • Paste suggested edits next to original sentences.
    • Ensure correct formatting and alignment.

  7. Task 7: Submission

    Document formatted correctly and double-checked before submission.
    • Review the Word document for formatting.
    • Log into the Moodle portal.
    • Upload and submit the document.
